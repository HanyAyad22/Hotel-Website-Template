Luxury Comfort

Located in the lovely area of DownTown, Cairo, enjoys a commanding position in the restaurants, shopping, culture hub of Egypt. From here, guests can make the most of all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the property offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

Pyramids is renowned for its quality services and friendly staff, and lives up to expectations. The property offers access to a vast array of services, including daily housekeeping, 24-hour front desk, car park, pets allowed, 24-hour check-in.

Rooms

268 guest rooms and 48 suites designed for the discerning traveller. Warm colour tones accented with rich textiles and dark woods create open and inviting spaces. Floor-to-ceiling windows provide sweeping vistas of the Cairo skyline and river. Modern conveniences and our signature amenities make every room a comforting place to unwind.

Restaurant

We introduce what suits the Egyptian taste and integrate between different cultures, by merging different Oriental and western recipes to make new dishes that are a fusion of the Eastern and the Western tastes with the overwhelming Egyptian touch.
