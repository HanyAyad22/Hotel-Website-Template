Explore The Place

Majestic views of the cityscape, the River, and Lakes. All housed in a skyscraper designed by many renowned architects. Situated downtown, our hotel is mere minutes from the Loop, Parks, Museums, Zoos, and Navy Pier. Discover the perfect blend of convenience and stylish comfort only at Pyramids Hotel. Visual and edible masterpieces that will delight and indulge. Discover contemporary Seasonal Egyptian cuisine. For a taste of our classic heritage, enjoy our Pyramids Afternoon Tea alongside views of Cairo in our stunning lounge.



268 guest rooms and 48 suites designed for the discerning traveller. Warm colour tones accented with rich textiles and dark woods create open and inviting spaces. Floor-to-ceiling windows provide sweeping vistas of the cairo skyline and river. Modern conveniences and our signature amenities make every room a comforting place to unwind.

About

The Pyramids’s legend dates back to 1865, when The Cairo, Egypt opened as Africa’s first 'Grand Hotel'. 1865 is Cairo Hospitality Group's way of expressing appreciation for valuable guest support to our collection of hotels.
